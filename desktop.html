Ferry Port Simulator

You are a hard-working clerk in the ferry port. Your goal is to take care of all vehicles that are coming to the port. You don't know the order of coming, it is random. You shouldn't let them enter the ferry if their fuel is below 10%. In case it is, you refuel it. Also, if a bigger vehicle comes, you can check its cargo by the vehicle door.

Technology: C# and WPF.
