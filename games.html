Games

Breakout

A clone of the popular game. The game has 3 levels. The player controls the paddle using the keyboard. The game is made in c++ using the allegro5 library.

Whac A Mole

Another AR mobile game. Everyone played this famous game. Now it has a modern and fresh look. The game has five regular levels plus boss level. There are 3 different enemies and weapons you can play with.

Dancing Hero

Augmented Reality mobile game. You can play with 5 heroes and choose different songs. The main goal is to collect all falling objects before time runs out. Every level unlocks a new hero. Game was made for fun and non-commercial use. All models are from sketchfab and songs are dowloaded from YouTube.

Revival of Cleo

Video game was presented as the final project for the conclusion of 7th generation educational program at the PISMO gaming incubator in Croatia. Genre of game is singleplayer story based shooter made in Unity. The game has a steampunk theme and a dystopian narrative.

Check out the game trailer here.

My Sweet Smothie

Hypercasul mobile game inspired by Juicy Slicer. You need to slice as much as possible fruits. If you gain enough coins you can buy different weapons in the shop. Also, you can upload your score in internet database

Another Zombie Game

There are tons of zombie games but...here is another. There is one regular level and the boss level. This game is made for fun and practical purpose.

Click City

The main goal is to survive from small village to megaopolis and reach 1 million people. You have 7 different stages in the game and 6 different buttons to manipulate the economics of the game.

Jungle Chase

Your greediness for lost treasure brought you to jungle. Enemies and traps are everywhere. Try to find your way out and collect some of lost treasure. There are 3 different levels and each of them has different enemies.
